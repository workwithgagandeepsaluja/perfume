Concentration Guide

Parfum / Extrait (20-30% Oil)

Lasts 10-12+ Hours. Best for long days.

Eau de Parfum (EDP) (15-20% Oil)

Lasts 7-9 Hours. The standard for clones.

Clone Terminology

Clone: A scent designed to smell 90%+ similar to a specific designer/niche fragrance.

Twist: Starts similar to the original but dries down into something unique (e.g., Afnan Turathi Blue).

Maceration: Many Middle Eastern clones need to sit in a dark cupboard for 2-4 weeks after the first few sprays to reach full potency.

Top Brands in India

Lattafa / Maison Alhambra: Best value for money. Huge variety.

Armaf: Higher price, but often "Beast Mode" performance (e.g., CDNIM).

Afnan: excellent blending quality, often smoother than Lattafa.
